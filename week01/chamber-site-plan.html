Site Purpose

Our chamber of commerce is committed to promoting economic development, supporting local businesses, and enhancing the overall business climate in Dessalines, Haiti.

This website serves as a platform to facilitate networking among local entrepreneurs, provide valuable resources, and promote business opportunities in our community.
