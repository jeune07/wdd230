Dessalines Chamber of commerce

The Dessalines Chamber of Commerce is a dynamic organization with a primary mission: to empower entrepreneurs on their journey to business growth and startup success. We are dedicated to facilitating connections between our members and business professionals worldwide. Our goal is to foster a thriving business ecosystem where opportunities flourish and businesses thrive.
